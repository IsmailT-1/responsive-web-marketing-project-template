What is SnowboardersUnite?

Do you want to go thrill seeking? Do you feel the energy shift when your board touches the snow? Does the adrenaline course through your veins when you feel the speed going up and down the slopes of planet earth, whether it be volcanic ash in Italy, grassy hills in Ireland or the obvious snowy mountains in Alberta? Whether you are a beginner or are veteran to snowboarding and skiing, everyone is welcome to join us at SnowboardersUnite. Our culture here is an all-inclusive environment that does not discriminate and are actively a safe space for all.

Time to get good!

Snowboarding can seem a little daunting, especially when you're a beginner to the beautiful sport. However, with our straight-forward tutorials, you'll go from zero to hero very quick. Whether you own a Burton snowboard or a snowboard from Canadian Tire, you'll be glad to have stumbled upon our page.
